The 2017's HackBrazil

HackBrazil is an annual competition to put together Brazilians best hackers and challenge them to make a difference in the country. The challenges that will foster Brazil’s development in the years to come, the Brazil Conference at Harvard & MIT is organised around four pillars, which every year will bring different problem statements to be addressed by its participants.

What are the challenges Brazil faces?

As a platform centred around an annual meeting to discuss the problems and promote the actions which will foster Brazil’s development in the years to come, the Brazil Conference at Harvard & MIT is organised around four pillars, which every year will bring different questions to be addressed by its participants.

 
People

Health, education, poverty, public safety. What Brazil has to teach and to learn about improving the life of ordinary people?

Globe

In a world increasingly integrated, where sustainability imposes itself as a global agenda, what is Brazil’s role in the international scenario?

Foundations

What are the political and economic institutional bases necessary for Brazil’s development?

Imagination

Technology, innovation, social and institutional experimentation. How can creativity change the country’s reality?

What answers does Brazil needs and who would you like to hear answering those questions?

Our Judges

Luciano Huck

Brazilian TV host celebrity, speacher and some of the Brazilian’s most dynamic investor and entrepreneur. Luciano is also an angel investor and has a lot of philanthropic initiatives.

Gustavo Roxo

Gustavo Roxo is the CIO of BTG Pactual in Brazil and angel investor for start-ups in Brazil. Prior to joining BTG, Gustavo was a partner at Mckinsey & Company. Gustavo was also CIO for Banco Real and Vice-President at Banco Santander.

Jorge Paulo Lemann

Brazilian businessman and symbol of meritocracy and efficient management. Lemann founded 3G Capital Group and led the merge of H.J. Heinz and Kraft Foods Group. He founded the largest brewery company in the world: Anheuser-Busch InBev.
